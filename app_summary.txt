MediLine is a smart Android-based doctor appointment and queue management system designed to eliminate long waiting times at local clinics. The app connects patients and doctors through a real-time digital queue — patients can browse nearby clinics, book appointments, upload their medical records, and track their exact position in the queue live from their phone, while doctors get a dedicated dashboard to manage their clinic, advance the queue, view patient medical records, and upload prescriptions directly from the app. Both walk-in and app-based patients are handled within a single unified queue, and the system fires automatic push notifications when a patient's turn is approaching or when a prescription has been uploaded. Built with Java on Android using Firebase Firestore for real-time data, Firebase Authentication for login (email/password and Google Sign-In), Cloudinary for image storage, and Google Maps for clinic location — MediLine replaces manual, paper-based clinic workflows with a transparent, efficient, and fully digital healthcare experience.
